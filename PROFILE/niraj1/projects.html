Rainfall Analysis of Kokrajhar-Dhubri District

A choropleth map shows rainfall in Kokrajhar and Dhubri districts using data from rainfall stations. Kriging and Inverse Distance Weighting (IDW) interpolation methods were applied. The map visualizes spatial rainfall distribution across these districts. It helps in analyzing regional precipitation patterns effectively.
